Our Services

Blood Donation

Blood donation is the process of giving blood voluntarily to help others. Blood donations are used to treat patients who have lost blood due to surgery, injury, or illness. Blood is also used to make blood products, such as red blood cells, platelets, and plasma.

Blood Testing

Blood testing is the process of analyzing blood to check for diseases, infections, and other medical conditions. Blood tests are also used to monitor the effectiveness of certain treatments.

Blood Transportation

A blood transfusion is a medical procedure in which blood is transferred from one person to another. Blood transfusions are used to treat patients who have lost blood due to surgery, injury, or illness.
